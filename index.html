Is your Birthday a Palindrome?


Understand Palindrome

Palindrome is a word, phrase, or sequence that reads the same backwards as forwards. For example: MOM, LOL, RACECAR, NITIN, 101, 404.

How our algorith works?

First it checks wheather your Birthday is a Palindrome or not.

It checks in all the different formats:

  • dd-mm-yyyy
  • mm-dd-yyyy
  • yyyy-mm-dd
  • dd-mm-yy
  • mm-dd-yy
  • yy-mm-dd

And if it's not then gives you the next Palindrome Date and the number of day by which you missed it
